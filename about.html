About the studio

A small studio with a big standard.

ASAI Technology is a digital engineering studio. We build premium websites, business automation, SEO and growth systems for founders, professionals and growing companies who care how their work looks — and how fast it ships. We're based in Noida, India, and work with clients across the country and around the world.

What we do

We're a full-stack digital partner, not a single-service vendor. We design and hand-code high-performance websites; we build business process automation that removes repetitive manual work from your day; we engineer growth infrastructure — technical SEO, analytics and content systems — that compounds month over month; and we ship internal tools and Chrome extensions that live inside the software your team already uses. Whether you need a single professional one-pager or an end-to-end system, it's all built under one roof.

How we work

We started in 2025 by building our own products first — including AI.Reply, a Gmail extension live on the Chrome Web Store. That taught us production discipline the hard way: real users, real reviews, real breakage at 2 AM. Every client project now gets that same standard. We keep the studio deliberately small, take on a limited number of projects at a time, and put a finished first draft in your hands within 24 hours of a brief — because momentum matters more than meetings. Everything is custom-built and hand-coded: no page builders, no template bloat, and no lock-in — you own the code, the accounts and the domain.

Who we work with

Because our work is digital, location is never a limit. We work remotely with clinics, law firms, consultants, retailers, cafés, fitness brands, startups and established companies — across India and internationally. If you can brief us over WhatsApp or a call, we can build for you, wherever you are.
