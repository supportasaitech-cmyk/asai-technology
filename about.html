About the studio

A small studio with a big standard.

ASAI Technology is a digital engineering studio based in Noida, India. We build websites, browser extensions, and automation for professionals and businesses that care how their work looks — and how fast it ships.

We started in 2025 by building our own products first — including AI.Reply, a Gmail extension live on the Chrome Web Store. That taught us production discipline the hard way: real users, real reviews, real breakage at 2 AM. Every client project now gets that same discipline. We keep the studio deliberately small, take on limited projects at a time, and put a finished draft in your hands within 24 hours of a brief — because momentum matters more than meetings.
