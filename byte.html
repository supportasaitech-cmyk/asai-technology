Meet BYTE.

He lives in the corner, watches your cursor, blinks, strolls, waves, naps when you're away, and cheers when you click him. Move your mouse, scroll, leave him alone for 20 seconds, or click him — he behaves differently every time.

How to test BYTE: move your cursor around (his eyes follow), wait a few seconds between actions (he picks random antics), stop touching everything for ~20s (he falls asleep with 💤), then move the mouse (he wakes), and click him for a happy reaction. Refresh for a different sequence each visit.
