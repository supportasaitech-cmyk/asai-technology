Brand board — logo, colors, templates

The problem

Meridian's three café locations posted on social media with three different visual styles — inconsistent colors, random fonts, and a logo existing in four unofficial variations. Customers couldn't tell the posts came from the same brand.

What we built

A complete identity system: a redrawn logo with usage rules, a documented color and typography system, and twenty ready-to-post templates the staff fill in themselves. Every location now posts from the same kit — consistent without a designer on call.
