SEO, explained like you're busy running a business.

By ASAI Technology · 17 July 2026 · 6 min read

Every business owner has received the spam call: "Sir, we will bring your website to Google first page." Most of what's sold as SEO in India is smoke. Here's what SEO actually is — in the plain words the callers avoid, because plain words can be checked.

What Google is actually doing

Google's job is answering questions with the best available page. When someone types "CA for GST filing in Ghaziabad", Google scans billions of pages asking three things: Can I read this site properly? Does it clearly answer this exact question? Do signals suggest it's trustworthy? SEO is simply making your site score well on all three. That's the entire field.

The 3 layers of real SEO

  1. Technical — can Google read you? Your site must load fast (Google measures this and ranks slow sites lower), work flawlessly on mobile, and include structured data — invisible labels telling Google "this is a dental clinic, at this address, open these hours." Verifiable: your developer can show you these in the code.
  2. Content — do you answer real searches? People don't search "welcome to our esteemed organisation." They search "cheque bounce lawyer Noida" and "tiffin service near Sector 62." Your pages need to contain the actual phrases your customers type, answered clearly.
  3. Trust — does the internet vouch for you? Google Business Profile with real reviews, consistent name/address/phone across the web, and mentions from other sites. Built steadily, not bought in bulk.

What changed in 2026: AI search (GEO)

A growing share of your customers now ask ChatGPT, Gemini, or Google's AI Overviews instead of scrolling links: "suggest a good interior designer in Noida." These AIs recommend businesses whose websites they can read and trust — clear descriptions, structured data, factual pages, even a special file (llms.txt) written for AI readers. This is called GEO, and businesses doing it now are being cited while competitors are invisible to the fastest-growing search channel.

Spotting fake SEO in one question: ask "what exactly will you change, and how do I verify it?" Real SEO has concrete, checkable answers — speed scores before/after, structured data you can test on Google's own tools, rankings tracked openly. Fake SEO answers with "backlinks and keywords, sir" and a monthly invoice.

What you can do this week, free

  • Create/claim your Google Business Profile and fill every field.
  • Ask 5 happy customers for Google reviews (send them the direct link).
  • Check your site's speed at PageSpeed Insights — under 2 seconds or you have work to do.
  • Read your homepage asking: "does this contain the words a customer would actually search?"

Frequently asked questions

What is SEO in simple words?

SEO is making your website easy for Google to find, understand, and recommend — so when someone searches for what you sell, you appear without paying for ads.

How long does SEO take to work?

Honest answer: 2–6 months for visible movement on competitive searches, faster for your own name and local searches. Anyone promising #1 in 30 days is selling you a story.

What is GEO or AI search optimization?

In 2026, people ask ChatGPT and Google's AI for recommendations. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is structuring your site's content so these AI systems can read, trust, and cite your business in their answers.
